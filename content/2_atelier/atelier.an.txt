About: Passionate about graphic design and tattooing, I discovered screen printing after several years of traveling. Once settled in Lyon, I spent eight years working in various workshops to master the technique, blending tradition and innovation. Today, I’ve decided to launch my own workshop to share this passion and offer original prints that combine precision and originality.
